Menu Highlights

Our menu features a variety of Mediterranean dishes, including fresh salads, hearty mains, and delicious desserts. Each dish is crafted with love and care, using seasonal ingredients to provide a unique dining experience every time you visit.

Meet the Owners

Little Lemon is owned by two Italian brothers, Mario and Adrian, who moved to the United States to pursue their shared dream of owning a restaurant. Mario relies on family recipes and his experience as a chef in Italy to craft the menu. Adrian does all the marketing for the restaurant and led the effort to expand the menu beyond classic Italian to incorporate additional cuisines from the Mediterranean region.
